My Projects

Word Scramble Game

•The Word Scramble Game is an educational program that improves vocabulary, spelling, and critical thinking through word-based challenges.

•It randomly selects a word from a chosen category, scrambles its letters, and asks the player to guess the correct word.

•Each correct answer increases the player’s score, while incorrect answers give immediate feedback by revealing the correct word.

•The game continues until all words in the selected category are used, ensuring no repetition during a session.
